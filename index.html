Meet Rebecca

Dedicated to Dogs
for Nearly Two Decades

I've been grooming dogs for 17 years, building a reputation across Mansfield for gentle, caring, and professional service. Every dog that comes through my door gets my complete, undivided attention.

Based in The Paddocks, my grooming environment is calm and relaxed — no crates, no rushing, no strangers. Just your dog and me.

I'm fully qualified and passionate about what I do. I take time to understand each dog's individual needs, temperament, and coat — so they leave looking fabulous and feeling happy.

  • Fully insured & City & Guilds qualified
  • Accepting puppies from any age
  • Experienced with all breeds & coat types
  • Patient with nervous & senior dogs
  • Dog first aid trained
What I Offer

Grooming Services

Full Groom
