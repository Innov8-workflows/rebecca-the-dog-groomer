Meet Rebecca

Dedicated to dogs for almost two decades

Rebecca has been grooming dogs for 17 years, building a reputation for gentle, caring, and professional service in The Paddocks, Mansfield. Every dog receives dedicated 1-to-1 attention in a calm, relaxed environment — no crates, no rushing, just a genuine home from home experience.

Fully qualified and passionate about what she does, Rebecca takes the time to understand each dog's individual needs, ensuring they leave looking fabulous and feeling happy.
